About Us

Too busy to cook? Working up late ? Sports Night ?
The word Bhoklagyo means craving for food . Anytime you feel the hunger or crave for a burger mixed with a fusion of 3 spicy sauces. Do remember us for our delicious food, ready to be delivered at your door step.
Bhoklagyo food delivery is the first late night delivery in Nepal , delivering foods within Kathmandu valley till 4 am in the morning!
Bhoklagyo foods is an online multi-cuisine production and delivery company. Our company promotes healthy and quality products for your well being. We are here to satisfy your food cravings with our fast food and provide you with our freshest ingredients made available by our highly experienced chef. You can order food from a variety of cuisine and we guarantee the quality of food.
To order, we provide both telecommunication and online food ordering facility. You can order from our social media sites or call us .
